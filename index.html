Koww is a magical cow trying to figure out whether the grass is greener on the other side of the chasm.


This is a port of the first known Quest game.


This port of The Adventures of Koww the Magician was created with QuestJS v1.4.0 and has IFID BBD5D89-78A2-4278-A85E-BC291A41FFE8.

Links to all the other versions have been provided, as well. You can play any version you like without downloading anything by following one of the 'Play In-Browser' links. You'll need to have Javascript enabled on your web browser.
